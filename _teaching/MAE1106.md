Served as a Graduate Teaching Assistant for an introductory aerospace engineering course combining lecture and hands-on laboratory instruction for first-year students. Supervised laboratory sessions involving atmospheric modeling, barometric altimetry, buoyant lift, low-speed wind tunnel experiments, and electric propulsion systems. Assisted students with experimental setup, data collection, and analysis, and evaluated quizzes and laboratory reports. Enforced laboratory safety procedures, supported team-based experimentation, and provided guidance on technical communication and professional practices in aerospace engineering.
